# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-03

This is the privacy policy for **CockRoach**, a product of **Layaa AI**
("we," "us"). It explains what we collect, why, how long we keep it,
and the rights you have over it.

## What we collect

- **Account data** — your email, display name, and avatar when you
  sign up.
- **Conversation content** — the messages you send Cockroach, plus
  the agent's responses, are stored in our database so you can revisit
  them. Decision logs, projects, artifacts, and memory items work the
  same way.
- **Usage telemetry** — token counts and request durations per call,
  used to monitor cost and performance. No conversation content is
  exposed in this telemetry.
- **Payment data** — handled by Stripe. We never see your card number;
  we store a Stripe customer ID and subscription status.

## What we do not do

- We **do not** sell your data.
- We **do not** train any model on your conversations.
- We **do not** share your projects with anyone outside Layaa AI's
  small operations team.

## Where data lives

- **Application data** — Supabase (PostgreSQL) hosted in the EU/US.
- **Inference** — Microsoft Azure OpenAI Service. Per Microsoft's
  contractual terms, your prompts are not used to train Azure or
  OpenAI models, are not retained after processing, and are not sent
  to OpenAI's consumer ChatGPT system.
- **Payments** — Stripe (when activated).

## Your rights

- **Export.** Use the in-app export buttons or email us; we'll send
  you a JSON archive within 14 days.
- **Delete.** Email `hello@layaa.ai` from your account email and we
  will delete your account and associated data within 30 days,
  except where retention is required by law.
- **Correct.** Most data is editable in-app. For anything else,
  email us.

## Cookies

We use a minimal session cookie to keep you logged in and a few
localStorage entries (notably for the onboarding-completed flag and
session UI preferences). We do not use third-party advertising
trackers or analytics that share with ad networks.

## EU / UK / Swiss residents (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Layaa AI is the data controller. Lawful basis: consent at signup
plus performance of the contract. You can lodge a complaint with
your local supervisory authority.

## California residents (CCPA)

You have the right to know, delete, and opt out of sale. We do not
sell personal information.

## Children

CockRoach is not directed to children under 16. If we learn we've
collected data on a child, we will delete it.

## Changes

We will notify users by email of any material change at least 30
days before it takes effect.

## Contact

Email `hello@layaa.ai` for any privacy question.
